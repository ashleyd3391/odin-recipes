Strawberry Pancakes


Description

This recipe will show you how to create delicious and fluffy strawberry pancakes!


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Almond Milk
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Mix your flour, baking soda, salt, and sugar into a bowl
  2. Whisk the egg and butter and add almond milk
  3. Puree strawberries in a blender
  4. Add to the mixture
  5. Add to non-stick pan on medium heat
  6. Drizzle with syrup and strawberries and enjoy!
