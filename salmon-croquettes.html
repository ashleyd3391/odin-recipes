Salmon Croquettes

Ingrediants

Directions

  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and cornmeal so it is evenly combined.
  • Add bell peppers, sweet onion, seasonings, Worcestershire sauce, mayonnaise, cilantro, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  • Shape into about 6-8 patties. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Once the skillet is hot, cook the patties for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  • Remove them to a paper towel lined plate or a warm oven until you are ready to serve.
