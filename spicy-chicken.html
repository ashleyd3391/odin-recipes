Spicy Chicken Sandwhich

Ingrediants

Directions

  • Mix seasonings into chicken. Form patties with damp hands. Place on baking paper and in freezer for 5 minutes.
  • Add breadcrumbs on plate, mix 1/2 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp paprika, and dash of cayenne pepper. Mix and coat patties.
  • Spray patties with light cooking oil spray and bake in oven at 200 degrees celsius for 15-18 mintues.
  • For sauce, mix mayonnaise, 2g mustard, 1/2 tsp garlic powder into a bowl.
  • Add patties to bun, add lettuce and sauce.
