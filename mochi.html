Mochi


Description

This recipe will show you how to create delicious sweet mochi!


Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Mix your rice flour and sugar into a bowl
  2. Pour in boiling water
  3. Stir until lumps disappear
  4. Cover container with plastic wrap and microwave on high
  5. Knead and coat
  6. Enjoy!
