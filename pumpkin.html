Pumpkin Pudding

Description

This recipe will show you how to create delicious pumpkin pudding!

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Pumpkin
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Blend egg, pumpkin, sugar, honey, and spices into a bowl
  2. Gradually add milk
  3. Place into pan
  4. Bake uncovered at 425 degrees for 10 minutes
  5. Serve warm or cold
  6. Enjoy!
