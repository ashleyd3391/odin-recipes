Beef Fried Rice

Ingrediants

Directions

  • Brown beef in large skillet over medium high heat 6-8 minutes, stirring to break up meat. Drain fat.
  • Stir in onions, celery, bean sprouts, mushrooms, bell peppers, and ginger. Cook over medium-high heat 5 minutes or until vegetables are crispy, stirring frequently.
  • Stir in rice and soy sauce. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Heat through, stirring occasionally.
